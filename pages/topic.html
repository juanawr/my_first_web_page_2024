Generative AI in Video Games

Introduction

Video games have evolved significantly in recent years, influencing the design of innovative technologies like Generative AI. This technology, which offers challenges, controlled environments, and instant feedback, has become more precise and efficient. Generative AI has become an essential part of the video game industry, enabling developers to create more realistic, immersive, and complex games. While it does not handle the entire development process, its presence is crucial, acting as a co-designer and contributing to the industry's growth and success.
